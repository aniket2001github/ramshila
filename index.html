OUR ABOUT US

Delivering Compassionate Care with Excellence and Integrity

Ramshila Healthcare Hospital has been a symbol of excellence and compassionate care since its inception. Located on Sakri-Pandaul Road in Madhubani, Bihar, it proudly stands as one of the most trusted multi-speciality hospitals in the region.

Spread over 19,000 sq. ft., our 100-bed facility is equipped with modern infrastructure, advanced diagnostics, and a dedicated team of highly qualified doctors and paramedics. We are committed to delivering high-quality, affordable healthcare with integrity.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: To deliver high-quality treatment, precise diagnostics, and 24/7 emergency care — accessible to all. We believe that healthcare is a right, not a privilege. That’s why we are especially committed to providing affordable healthcare solutions for those who may not have access to costly treatments elsewhere.
